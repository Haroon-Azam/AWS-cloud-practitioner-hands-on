# AWS Billing & Budgets

## Objective
To monitor AWS spending and prevent unexpected charges by configuring **AWS Budgets and cost alerts**.

---

## AWS Services Used
- AWS Billing and Cost Management
- AWS Budgets

---

## Tasks Performed
- Created a monthly cost budget
- Set spending thresholds to trigger alerts
- Configured email notifications for budget limits
- Reviewed AWS Free Tier usage dashboard

---

## Why Billing & Budgets is Important
AWS follows a pay-as-you-go model. Setting budgets and alerts helps:
- Prevent unexpected costs
- Monitor usage proactively
- Practice responsible cloud cost management

---

## Outcome
- Successfully configured budget alerts
- Gained visibility into AWS spending and Free Tier usage

---

## Notes
- Alerts ensure early notification before costs increase
